RN Design

We tell your story

Santuary. Belonging. Harmony.

Let me help you create your unique style using colours, patterns, textures and forms.

I believe that home should be a sanctuary for the soul. My aim is to create spaces with a sense of belonging and harmony; a place that tells your story.. Any design project can be daunting and naturally we would like to get things right. I can help you with this process, making it as stress free as possible, and turning it into an exciting adventure of creating your unique style using colours, patterns, textures and forms.

About Me: Before my interior design consultancy I had a successful career in the insurance industry. However I have always been fascinated and drawn to all different forms of art and visual aesthetics. This helped me to realise that my passion is to create beautiful spaces for people. I decided to follow my dream and gained professional qualifications in interior design which I completed with 1st Class BA (Hons) Degree.

Initial Consultation: It is our first informal meeting to chat about what you would like to achieve. It is about listening and interpreting your ideas in order to develop the design brief. I will also answer any question you may have about my services, timescale and fees. Our initial meeting is free of charge.

Design Concept: I will proceed with a survey or acquire all relevant drawings in order to develop the concept design. This includes spatial planning, colour schemes and creation of visuals, mood boards and samples to showcase the proposal. Once the concept design concept is agreed we can put the plan into motion.

Technical Drawings: If building alterations have been agreed I will produce detailed technical drawings for the purpose of pricing by constructors and for the planning authority.

Project co-ordination: I will meet regularly with the client and any contractors to oversee that the project is progressing according to plan until completion and hand over to the client.
